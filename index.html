Our Product

Campfirestix was created to solve an age old problem. The fire is cracklin', and you're in great company. You lose focus of your prize and it ends in spoils or debris-picking.

Why not use a skewer? The poorly designed skewers we are familiar with often result in the same fate! The poor design and material quality often results in a cheap, flimsy skewer difficult to control to achieve that idillic golden-brown. Using low-grade stainless steel allows the metal to bend (especially when heated), which is where gravity takes over. Aunt Sue! I dropped another one...

Our Story

Campfirestix began on the shores of Georgian Bay. Situated by the lake, the Swiderski's created countless Hallmark memories at their family cottage. Many of these memories involve camping on the beach roasting marshmallows and hot dogs. After consistently running into issues finding the ideal stick, or a functional skewer, Old Man Swiderski took the challenge to his work bench.

Essentially being born with a Campfirestix in his hand, Ryan Swiderski felt all campers could benefit from a more refined design. His pursuit naturally began on the shores of Georgian Bay, as they had when he was a child.

We only use Food Grade (304) Stainless Steel, not the cheaper alternative that is known to rust #SayNoToTetanus. The handle of each Campfirestix is sustainably collected driftwood that has naturally washed ashore the beautiful Georgian Bay. No two Campfirestix are the same. Our logo is only imprinted upon final inspection.Our logo on the handle is the final step in our Quality Assurance process. Your Campfirestix are also guaranteed for life!
